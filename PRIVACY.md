# Privacy Policy – BibleWish

Effective Date: January 2026

BibleWish respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, track, or
share any personal data.

## Information Collection

BibleWish does not collect:
- Personal information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Identifiers
- Accounts or profiles

All reading progress, bookmarks, and preferences are stored locally on your
device only.

## Third-Party Services

BibleWish does not use third-party services, analytics, advertising networks,
or tracking technologies.

## Children’s Privacy

BibleWish does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
The app is safe to use for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If the privacy practices of BibleWish ever change, this Privacy Policy will be
updated accordingly.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on
the project’s GitHub repository.

---

BibleWish is provided freely, without ads, subscriptions, or data monetization.
